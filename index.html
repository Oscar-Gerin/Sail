SHORT PRESENTATION OF SAILING :

Sailing is a sport that is increasingly practiced since the beginning of the 21st century. France is lucky enough to be almost surrounded by oceans and seas which offers a huge playground for the French. It is a sport that is extremely vast with many supports. For example, you can find supports such as: - the catamaran or the optimist, which are intended for young people, even though it is an Olympic discipline. - There are also pleasure boats for those who sail for pleasure, with friends at weekends or on holiday. - Adrenalin sports such as windsurfing or kite-surfing. Indeed, these are very light supports which allow to reach important speeds rather easily. - Finally, there is the field of ocean racing. This is a field that includes numerous races with more or less long courses with real experts who make their dreams come true. The aim of these races is, for example, to sail around the world or a more coastal course such as the Tour de France a la voile. The world of ocean racing has seen the emergence of many races, some of which have become legendary.
